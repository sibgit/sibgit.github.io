Habitat and distribution

It is well known in France and francophone regions of Switzerland and Italy. It loves the mountains and can be found in the pyrenees.

Diet

Mountain goats eat a diet that consists mostly of grasses, herbs, sedges, mosses, lichen, bark and twigs. They are herbivores classified as ruminants. This means they acquire nutrients from plant-based food by fermenting it in their stomach prior to digestion through microbial action.
