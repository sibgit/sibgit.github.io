Pallas's Cat

Habitat and distribution

The Pallas's cat lives in high altitude grasslands and shrublands with low snow coverage.Its presence has been recorded in the Caucasus, Iranian Plateau, Hindu Kush, parts of the Himalayas, Tibetan Plateau, Altai-Sayan region and South Siberian Mountains.

Diet

The pallas's cat feeds on cute little furry animals that are unlucky enough to be smaller than their predator.
