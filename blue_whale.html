Habitat and distribution

Arctic, Atlantic, Indian and Pacific Oceans, from the tropics to the drift ice of polar waters.

Diet

?? Eats krill.

Behavior

Blue whales are migrators: they spend summers feeding in polar waters and as winter arrives they migrate towards the equatorial water. Thy swim at about 5 miles an hour but they can be as fast as 20 miles per hour if needed (but only for a short time).
